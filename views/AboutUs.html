About Us

Universe Mines is an established Stockholder, Exporter, Supplier of Alloy Steel, Stainless Steel, Carbon, Steel, Nickel Alloys, Duplex, Super duplex and various other exotic Ferrous & Non-Ferrous Metal products such as Sheet , Plate ,Coil , Pipes, Tubes And Pipe Fittings.

Universe Mines an ISO CERTIFIED COMPANY started in 1996 and now we are an authorized Stockholder of one of the best exporter in the world with state of the art warehousing, integrated ERP system and a tie-up with Internationally-reputed third party inspection agencies (TPIA). We regularly fulfill the orders in large volumes and have been working with unparallel earnestness and zeal.

As markets today demand better performance, more reliability & higher quality. We are expanding to new areas on the map, offering a wide range of products & services than even before.
